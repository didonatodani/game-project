Sugar Swoop
How to play

Welcome to Candy Shooter Adventure, a thrilling single-player game where you navigate a whimsical world filled with candy-themed challenges! Inspired by the vibrant aesthetics of Wreck-It Ralph's Sugar Rush, this game combines engaging gameplay with delightful visuals to create an exciting gaming experience.

Objective

Dodge or shoot the enemies that come from the bottom of the screen. Collect sweets for points and hearts to stay in the game longer.

Movement

Move your character using the arrow keys or WASD.

Shooting

Press the spacebar to shoot peppermint bullets.

Enemies

Cotton Candy Cloud

Slow you down and remove points.

Candy Bolts

Significantly slow you down and remove points.

Candy Bandits

Make you lose a life.

Power-ups

Candy Coins

Collect for 10 points.

Lollipop Tokens

Collect for 5 points.

Candy Hearts:

Collect for an extra life.

Scoring

Gain points by collecting sweets. Stay in the game longer by collecting hearts for extra lives.
